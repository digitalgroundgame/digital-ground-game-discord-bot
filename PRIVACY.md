# Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Digital Ground Game Discord bot ("the bot") handles information when it is invited to, configured for, or used in a Discord server. This document is a basic policy for project transparency and is not legal advice.

## Information We Collect

The bot may collect and store information needed to provide its features, including:

- Discord-generated IDs, such as user IDs, guild IDs, channel IDs, message IDs, role IDs, and event IDs
- Server configuration and user preferences
- Command usage data needed to process bot commands
- Calendar, attendance, onboarding, welcome-thread, integration, and content-related data submitted to or created through the bot
- Logs and operational metadata needed to run, debug, secure, and improve the bot

The bot may temporarily cache Discord data while processing events or commands. Cached data may be removed automatically after it is no longer needed or when the bot leaves a server.

## How We Use Information

Collected information is used to operate and maintain the bot, including:

- Processing commands and Discord events
- Managing server-specific settings
- Supporting user preferences and account linking
- Running attendance, calendar, onboarding, and content features
- Debugging errors, preventing abuse, and monitoring service health
- Producing aggregate or statistical information, such as server counts or usage totals

## Sharing Information

The bot may share limited information with third-party services when required for bot functionality, such as Discord, Google Calendar, Google Groups, or other configured integrations.

The bot may share non-sensitive aggregate or statistical data. Personally identifying information, other than Discord-generated IDs needed for bot features and integrations, is not sold.

## Data Retention

Data is retained for as long as needed to provide bot features, maintain operational records, resolve abuse or security issues, or comply with applicable obligations.

Some Discord-generated IDs may be retained after other data is removed when needed for statistics, auditability, security, or bot functionality.

## Data Removal

Server owners may request removal of server-specific data. Users may request removal of personal data associated with their Discord account where removal is technically possible and does not interfere with required operational, security, or legal records.

To protect server and user data, requests may require proof that the requester owns or controls the relevant Discord account or server.

## Children

The bot is not intended for use by individuals who are not old enough to use Discord under Discord's Terms of Service.

## Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the bot after an update means you accept the updated policy.

## Contact

Privacy questions and data requests may be made through the official support server or other support channels provided by the bot maintainers.
